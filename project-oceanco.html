The Challenge & Execution

Capturing the sheer scale and luxury of the Leviathan required a production strategy that balanced high-end cinematic aesthetics with agile documentary-style shooting. The brief demanded a visual language that felt both intimate and grandiose.

We deployed a hybrid team, utilizing heavy-lift drones for dynamic exteriors and specialized gimbal setups to navigate the yacht's intricate interiors without compromising the majestic spatial feel. Post-production focused on a rigorous color grading process to ensure the final output reflected the brand's premium positioning—a high-contrast, moody, yet inviting atmosphere.
